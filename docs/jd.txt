About the Role

We are looking for a motivated AI/ML Intern who is passionate about Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and Data Science. You will work alongside experienced engineers to build, train, evaluate, and deploy machine learning models while contributing to real-world AI-powered applications.

This internship is ideal for students or recent graduates looking to gain practical industry experience in AI and machine learning.

Responsibilities
Collect, clean, and preprocess structured and unstructured datasets.
Build and train machine learning and deep learning models.
Analyze datasets to identify trends and insights.
Implement data preprocessing and feature engineering pipelines.
Evaluate model performance using appropriate metrics.
Deploy ML models using APIs or cloud platforms.
Assist in developing AI-powered applications and automation tools.
Optimize model performance and inference speed.
Research new AI techniques and present findings to the team.
Write clean, maintainable, and well-documented code.
Collaborate with software engineers, product managers, and designers.
